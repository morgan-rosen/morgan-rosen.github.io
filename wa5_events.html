Upcoming Events

Open Farm Day – Saturday, Sept 28

Time: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM

Age Range: 5–18 years

Join us for a full day at the farm! Kids can interact with animals, explore gardens, and participate in fun outdoor activities. Staff and volunteers will be on hand to provide support and guidance. Families are encouraged to bring water, snacks, and comfortable clothing for outdoor play.

Animal Care Workshop – Saturday, Oct 5

Time: 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Age Range: 7–18 years

This workshop teaches kids basic animal care skills, including feeding, grooming, and understanding animal behavior. Participants will gain hands-on experience in a safe and supportive environment. Volunteers will assist with activities to ensure every child feels included and confident.

Sensory-Friendly Harvest Event – Saturday, Oct 12

Time: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Age Range: 5–18 years

This special event is designed to be sensory-friendly, with quieter spaces and accommodations for children who may be sensitive to noise or crowds. Kids will participate in pumpkin picking, crafts, and harvest-themed activities. Staff and volunteers are available to provide support and make adaptations as needed.
