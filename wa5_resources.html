Resources for Families & Volunteers

Event Calendars & Sign-Ups

Stay up to date with all Inclusive Acres events, including our weekly Open Farm days, special workshops, and seasonal activities. Here, you can register your child for events online and receive confirmation emails to keep track of participation.

Activity Guidelines

Each activity is designed for neurodivergent youth ages 5–18. We provide detailed guidelines about what children can expect, what to bring, and how to prepare for hands-on activities with animals and outdoor exploration. This helps parents feel confident that each experience is safe and supportive.

Accommodations & Support

Inclusive Acres ensures that all children can participate comfortably. Information about sensory accommodations, special assistance, and volunteer support is included for each event. Parents can contact staff in advance if their child requires specific adaptations.

Family & Volunteer Portal

Families can create accounts to view past event participation, track upcoming activities, and receive reminders. Volunteers can register for shifts, check schedules, and communicate with staff to stay engaged in the farm’s programs.
