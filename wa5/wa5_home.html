Welcome to Inclusive Acres

Inclusive Acres provides neurodivergent youth with a safe and fun environment to play, explore, and interact with animals while building confidence and friendships.

  • Open Farm Days with friendly animals
  • Inclusive nature trails and sensory gardens
  • Workshops and seasonal events for all abilities
