Upcoming Events

Open Farm Day – Saturday, Sept 28

Time: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM

Age Range: 5–18 years

Join us for a full day at the farm! Kids can interact with animals, explore gardens, and participate in fun outdoor activities. Families are encouraged to bring water, snacks, and comfortable clothing for outdoor play.

Animal Care Workshop – Saturday, Oct 5

Time: 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Age Range: 7–18 years

This workshop teaches kids basic animal care skills, including feeding, grooming, and understanding animal behavior. Participants will gain hands-on experience in a safe and supportive environment.

Sensory-Friendly Harvest Event – Saturday, Oct 12

Time: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Age Range: 5–18 years

This special event is designed to be sensory-friendly, with quieter spaces and accommodations for children who may be sensitive to noise or crowds. Activities include pumpkin picking, crafts, and harvest-themed fun.
